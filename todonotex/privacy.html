1. Overview

DreamForge Nexus (hereinafter referred to as "Company") recognizes the importance of protecting your personal information and complies with the "Personal Information Protection Act" and related laws. This Privacy Policy explains the types of personal information collected by the TodoNoteX application (hereinafter referred to as "the App"), the purposes of collection and use, provision to third parties, and destruction procedures.

2. Personal Information Collected

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect the following information through Firebase services:

  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, screen view frequency, feature usage statistics
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version
  • Error Reports: App crash and error logs (Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Anonymous Identifiers: Randomly generated identifiers that do not identify users

2.2 Information NOT Collected

The App does NOT collect the following information:

  • Names, addresses, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers
  • Location information
  • Contact information
  • Data from camera or microphone

3. Purpose of Collection and Use

The Company uses collected personal information for the following purposes:

  • Service Improvement: Optimizing app performance and enhancing user experience
  • Error Fixing: Ensuring service stability through crash and bug analysis
  • Statistical Analysis: Service development through usage pattern analysis
  • New Feature Development: Planning new features based on user needs

4. Provision to Third Parties and Outsourcing

4.1 Google LLC (Firebase)

The App uses Firebase services from Google LLC. Anonymized data collected is transmitted to Google for the following purposes:

  • Firebase Analytics: Usage statistics analysis
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Error reporting and analysis

Google takes appropriate technical and physical security measures to protect data. For details, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.

4.2 Data Outsourcing

The Company outsources personal information processing as follows:

Service Provider Outsourced Tasks Duration
Google LLC App analytics, error reporting Service provision period

5. Retention and Destruction of Personal Information

5.1 Retention Period

  • Firebase Data: Follows Google's data retention policy (up to 26 months)
  • Local Data: Stored on device until user deletes the app

5.2 Destruction Method

  • Firebase Data: Automatically deleted according to Google's deletion policy
  • Local Data: Automatically deleted when app is removed, or can be manually deleted by user

6. Data Storage Location

6.1 Local Storage

All Todo data in the App is stored in an SQLite database on the user's device. This data is not transmitted outside the device and is completely under the user's control.

6.2 Backup and Restore

Users can export their data as backup files (Export) or import data from backup files (Import). These backup files are managed by the user and are not transmitted to any server.

7. Security Measures

The Company takes the following measures to ensure the security of personal information:

  1. Technical Measures: Use of encrypted communication (HTTPS) protocol
  2. Managerial Measures: Minimization of personnel handling personal information and regular training
  3. Physical Measures: Physical access control to data storage

8. Rights of Data Subjects

Users have the following rights:

  1. Right to Access: Request access to collected personal information
  2. Right to Rectify/Deletion: Request correction or deletion of incorrect information
  3. Right to Restrict Processing: Request suspension of personal information processing
  4. Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for Firebase Analytics collection (available in app settings)

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the contact information below.

9. Protection of Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from children under the age of 14. Legal guardians of children under 14 may request access, correction, or deletion of their child's personal information by contacting us at the contact information below.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be changed due to changes in laws and government policies. In case of changes, we will provide notice at least 7 days in advance through in-app notices or our website.
